About the Club

We are students who believe architecture is one of the most human acts — shaping the spaces where life happens.

Clark Architecture Club was founded by a small group of students who wanted more than what the standard curriculum offered. We wanted to draw, build, critique, and imagine — together. Over three years, we've grown into one of Clark's most active design-focused organizations.

Our membership spans every grade and every level of experience. Some members are set on attending architecture school; others just love the creative process. We welcome both. What we share is a fascination with the built environment and how it shapes human experience.

We are rooted in Las Vegas — a city that is itself one of the most architecturally audacious places on earth. We take that as both inspiration and responsibility.

Make things by hand

Sketching, drafting, and model-making keep our ideas physical and honest. We start with pencil before pixels.

Critique generously

Good design comes from honest feedback. We give and receive it as gifts, not attacks.

Design for community

Architecture that doesn't serve people is just sculpture. We keep the human in every project we take on.

Be curious, always

We ask why a building stands the way it does, who it serves, and what it says. Then we ask it again.
